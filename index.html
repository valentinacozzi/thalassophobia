The Project

The project has been carried out for the exam of Electronic Publishing and Digital Storytelling held by Professor Marilena Daquino for the a.a. 2021-2022, within the Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge master's degree at the University of Bologna.

The general aim of the project is to investigate topics related to the field of art history, exploiting linked open data as much as possible and making use of the information available on Wikidata.

The focus of the project is the genre of portraiture (portraits and self-portraits) in a period span of 70 years: from 1870 to 1950. In particular, our study focuses on the role that color plays in the representation of humans and the change in the usage of colour in the different modern art's movements.
